For Task 1 :

 - I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. 
 
 - Canva is an easy-to-use online design tool that provides a variety of templates, icons, and layouts 
suitable for beginners.

 - In my infographic, I included key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet 
practices, and maintaining a professional online presence. I explained digital literacy as the a
bility to effectively and responsibly use digital technologies. I also highlighted important safety
measures such as using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links. Additionally, I included tips on 
building a professional online identity, which is important for students in today’s digital world.

 - One interesting part of this task was designing the layout and selecting suitable icons to represent 
each concept. The challenging part was organizing the information clearly within limited space while keeping
the design visually appealing.
